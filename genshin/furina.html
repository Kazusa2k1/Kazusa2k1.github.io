My impression of her

The first time I saw Furina was during the main storyline in version 4.0. She appeared in front of Lumine as a smug and sassy Hydro Archon right when the traveler arrived at Fontaine. She sat on the 2nd floor of the tower, looking like a boss trying to intimidate her. The two companions also noticed that instead of reverence, the citizens considered her an idol or celebrity, quite different from the image of a normal Archon. Next, we got a glimpse of Furina's interaction with Neuvillete. During this dialogue, she was terrified of having to meet Arlecchino alone, as she was incredibly afraid of the Knave. The next scene covered how this conversation went. Arlecchino harbored some doubt whether Furina was actually the true Archon, since she did not display the powers that people of her caliber should. In all of these dialogues, the Archon seemed quite flustered, often not sure how to answer.
