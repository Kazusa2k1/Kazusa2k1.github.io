My impression of her

The first time I saw Arlecchino was in a preview video of the Fatui on Youtube. Throughout the whole video, no-one was noticeable except for her, which was confirmed by a majority of other players, not just me. I had to wait quite a while for her next appearance in the game, which occurred in version 4.0. In the main quest, Arlecchino was introduced as the Knave from the Fatui Harbingers. Undeniably, she had a intimidating aura that scared Furina to her core, especially when she secretly approached the Hydro Archon in the middle of the street. I remember having felt so bad for her at the time! Arlecchino appeared a few more times during the story, one particular scene was when she praised Furina for her unbelievable 500 years of keeping Focalors hidden behind the scene.

A lot of hype came when Genshin Impact released Arlecchino's drip marketing on social media. Everyone really looked forward to rolling for her in 4.6, including me. When Chiori came home and I finished farming all of her materials, I decided to focus on pre-farming for the Knave. Not so fortunately this time, her ascention and talent materials can only be obtained in the upcoming boss fight so I had to wait. I could only collect all of the rainbow roses beforehand as well as prepared the Fatui materials, which I had an abundance of beforehand. At the end of 4.5, the pre-version ritual began, the only exception was a huge capacity required for the update - a total of 30gb on my laptop! As I'm writing this, Arlecchino's banner will come the day after tomorrow so I literally cannot wait.

Fun fact: Arlecchino has an A3 in my room, in the middle of the main wall, next to Morgan from FGO
