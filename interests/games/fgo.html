My experience

FGO was the first gacha game I've ever played, before knowing that this genre existed. Back then, I even decided that I would not play another one since I was too busy with this game, until Genshin Impact won over my temptation. This period lasted around 3 years, starting from early 2018 when I finished watching the Fate series. I was instantly hooked with the storyline, where Gudao and Mash had to travel across singularities and lostbelts in order to save the world. The journey in each of them has never been easy, but they managed to overcome everything in just the slightest margin. The enemies always seemed inexplicably strong, but thanks to servants they formed pacts with along the way, they were able to achieve victories everytime. Each journey was exhilirating in its own way, and until now I've always been invested in the story.

Gacha is another advantage of Fate/Grand Order. The game has a great deal of characters, including numerous waifus that I want to roll for. The crazy thing about FGO is that I've actually lost the account twice, thanks to the game's system of transferring data. Lasengle only allows players to use code transfers, not logging in via an account or social media. The first account I had lasted from 2018 until 2020, when I dropped my phone while riding my bike, having forgotten to save the code beforehand. The account had a lot of my favorite characters from the beginning, including . The second time I lost my account was when I started playing FGO on Bluestacks. I figured playing on an Android emulator was a better idea since the laptop screen was much larger than my mobile. Nevertheless, in October of 2022, my old Dell laptop absolutely crashed so I had to get it fixed, while having forgotten to back everything up beforehand.

The most unbelievable fact is that Ryougi Shiki is the only character who has shown up for all of my accounts!
