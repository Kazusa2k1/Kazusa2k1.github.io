My experience

I first found out about Honkai Star Rail in December 2022, when the game was in the beta testing phase. I was so hyped, since I knew how good Genshin Impact was. As a result, I had a lot of expectation for this upcoming game. No more news about HSR came until the introduction livestream about a month before the game launch. Throughout the video, I was incredibly thrilled about everything: the plot, characters, art, music and more. The only aspect that I still want more til this day is the gameplay. Oh, how I wish the game allows us to directly control the characters like Genshin Impact. Just imagine we can use Kafka with guns or enable Robin to sing. That would be awesome!

One key difference between Honkai Star Rail and Genshin Impact is the setting between the two worlds. Genshin is based in the past settings, where technology is not as advanced as today. Instead, people from Teyvat rely on magic and their elemental powers. Also, the game is based in one world only, with different nations such as Fontaine, Inazume, Liyue, etc. which symbolize the 7 elements. On the other hand, HSR can be considered a science-fiction, with technologies that might happen in the future. The game takes place in multiple worlds in the universe, white the Astral Express travels to one planet at a time.

Characters in HSR are incredibly likable, which is such a crucial factor in gacha games. Developers have to make it so that players want to roll for their favorite ones. So far, I've liked all of the girls and succeeded in bringing home all of them. Their designs are exquisite, animations amazing along with good voice lines.
