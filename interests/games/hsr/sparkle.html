My impression of her

I knew Sparkle from HSR's trailer just before 2.0. I was really looking forward to this version, since it introduced Penacony - a totally new area. In the trailer, she is seen as a prankster similar to Hu Tao in Genshin Impact. She also has the ability to copy the appearance of anyone she knows, and her act is so convincing that others hardly ever find out. In the main story, she constantly makes use of this feature in order to surprise the Trailblazer and everyone around. She plays a major role in Penacony, often the type to disturb the peaceful atmosphere but also helps the main group.

The funniest thing about Sparkle is her ATK animation outside combat. She literally lifts her legs up high and kicks the sh out of the opponent, an incredibly elegant move. She can also hack the number of skill points available so they can never be out.
